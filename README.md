# 🛒 Food Mart Retail Sales Analysis | Power BI

## 📌 Project Overview

This project analyzes retail sales data from Food Mart using Power BI to provide actionable business insights into sales performance, customer behavior, product profitability, and store operations.

The dashboard helps decision-makers monitor KPIs, identify sales trends, evaluate product performance, and optimize business strategies through interactive visualizations.

---

# 🎯 Business Objectives

- Monitor overall sales performance
- Analyze revenue and profit trends
- Identify top-selling products
- Evaluate store performance
- Understand customer purchasing behavior
- Track sales by product category
- Support strategic business decisions
